## How to produce this

1. **ElevenLabs** (elevenlabs.io) — free account, paste each section,
   download MP3s. Voice "Rachel" (professional female).
2. **Phone scene** — film yourself with a second phone or laptop webcam.
   Phone on speaker, camera captures the phone ringing + you picking up.
   This is the money shot — judges need to SEE the phone ring.
3. **Screen recording** — Loom or OBS, no mic. Walk through: hub page →
   monitor → Gmail → Claude Code → dashboard.
4. **Combine** — CapCut (free, capcut.com): layer screen recording +
   ElevenLabs voiceover + phone video clip.
5. **Speed up** slow parts (Apify crawling, /vigil scanning) to 4x.
6. **Pause voiceover** during phone scene — let judges hear the actual call.
7. Total: ~45 min to produce.
